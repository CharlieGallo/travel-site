Visit Lagos

Enjoy fresh food, beautiful scenery, and great activities in the Algarve, Portugal.

Top three activities to do in Lagos

Sea Cave Tour

Take a kayak or boat to explore the beautiful caves for a day. A treat well worth it!

Marina de Lagos

Take in the great selection of boats at the marina while you enjoy fresh food and good wine in the variety of restaurants.

Lagos Town Square

Take a stroll around the cobbled streets to find a number of hidden gems and street entertainers.
